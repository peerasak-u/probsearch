About ProbSearch

A free tool built to help entrepreneurs discover real problems worth solving.

What is ProbSearch?

ProbSearch is a free search query generator that helps you discover user pain points, questions, and insights from social platforms like Reddit, Facebook, Pantip, and Twitter. Instead of manually crafting complex Google search operators, simply enter your topic and let the tool build optimized queries for you.

Why We Built This

Finding genuine user problems is the foundation of building successful products. But manually searching through social platforms to find these insights is time-consuming and requires knowledge of advanced search operators. We built ProbSearch to make this process faster and more accessible for entrepreneurs, product managers, and market researchers.
